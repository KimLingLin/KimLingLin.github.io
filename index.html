Our Story

In 2020, the pandemic swept through the Philippines like a storm. For many, this period was filled with uncertainty and anxiety. However, it was during this tumultuous time that the fates of two individuals named KAI intertwined.

The two brand founders, feeling the pressure of their companies being severely impacted, found themselves in the same animal adoption center by chance. There, they not only saw countless abandoned dogs but also felt the loneliness and helplessness in each other's hearts. At that moment, they decided to use love and courage to change everything, both to provide a warm home for those little lives and to find a glimmer of hope for themselves.

After adopting a puppy named "AKA," they discovered that companionship is not only warmth but also a source of strength. During these difficult days, AKA gave them endless joy and comfort. The friendship between the two KAIs gradually warmed up through daily companionship, supporting each other and overcoming difficulties together.

To spread this warmth to more people, they jointly founded AKIIKA Pets. The brand name was inspired by their names: KAI rearranged into AKIIKA, symbolizing rebirth and new hope. AKIIKA Pets is not just a pet product brand, but a story about love and companionship. Each product carries their love for pets and perseverance in life, aiming to provide the best care and protection for every pet.

At AKIIKA Pets, we believe that love is the most powerful force. Every family that chooses our products is a partner in spreading love and warmth with us. Let's work together to create a better future for those little lives that have experienced loneliness.
