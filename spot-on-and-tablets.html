AKIIKA Spot On and Tablets

Product Description

AKIIKA Spot On and Tablets offer comprehensive health care for your pets. Our spot-on treatment and tablets work together to prevent and repel parasites, ensuring your pet's overall well-being.

Key Features:

  • Effective against fleas, ticks, and other parasites
  • Easy to apply spot-on treatment
  • Convenient tablet form for internal parasite control
  • Suitable for both cats and dogs
  • Veterinarian-recommended formula

How to Use:

Apply the spot-on treatment to your pet's skin between the shoulder blades once a month. Administer tablets as directed by your veterinarian, typically once every 1-3 months.
